Formulas used
Step 1 — A_sat (averaged over depth range at radius R): A_sat = mean over d in [d_min, d_max] of ( Fe · P_Fe(R, d) + Ni · P_Ni(R, d) ) Step 2 — A_meas from the spike: N_spike = m_spike · C_spike · Nₐ / (1000 · M_Cl) N_36Cl = N_spike · (36Cl/35Cl) A_meas = N_36Cl · ln 2 · 1000 / (365 · 24 · 60 · T_½ · m_metal) [dpm/kg] Step 2 — Age and 1σ: t = (T_½ / ln 2) · ln( (A_sat − A_T) / (A_meas − A_T) ) σ_t² = (T_½ / ln 2)² · [ (σ_Asat/A_sat)² + (σ_mass/m_metal)² + (σ_spike/m_spike)² + (dA/100)² + (σ_Cspike/C_spike)² ] + ( (T_½/ln 2 + t) · σ(T_½)/T_½ )² Constants: Nₐ = 6.022×10²³ ; ln 2 = 0.6931… ; dA is the percent uncertainty on the 36Cl/35Cl ratio.
